How to Disable GlobalProtect (FBISD)

1. Restart your computer.
2. Log on to your computer as fast as you possibly can
(If you don't then, GlobalProtect may launch before you are able to.)
3. Launch TXSecureBrowser
4. Wait 120 seconds. (2 minutes)
5. Close TXSecure Browser
6. Once the desktop loads up, open TXSecure once again.
7. Close TXSecure after it loads up.

After your the computer restarts, GlobalProtect will most likely come back.

Optional Checks

Click on the small arrow pointing up on the taskbar.
Check if you see GlobalProtect.
(If it's not there, you were successful.)
(If it is, then let me know, by messaging me on discord | @repxility)
